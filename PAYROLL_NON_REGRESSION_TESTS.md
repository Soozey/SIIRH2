# Payroll Non Regression Tests

## Scope

Protect:

- formulas
- rates
- payroll preview lines
- imports
- reporting reuse of payroll results

## Minimum Cases

1. Base salary only worker
2. Worker with CNaPS/SMIE/FMFP overrides
3. Worker with imported HS/HM
4. Worker with imported absences and advances
5. Worker with dynamic prime formulas
6. Worker with payroll prime overrides
7. Worker with leave and permission summaries
8. Bulk preview with organizational filters
9. Reporting totals matching preview totals
10. Termination month historical reconstruction not crashing
